About BacklogBuster

BacklogBuster is a **student-focused platform** designed to help students clear **backlogs efficiently** and become **eligible for placements**. Our goal is to **simplify learning** and provide students with the right tools to **pass their exams with confidence**.

How Does BacklogBuster Help?

  • 📌 **Identifies weak areas** and provides focused study materials
  • 📌 **Saves time** by providing condensed & relevant content
  • 📌 **Reduces stress** by giving a structured learning approach
  • 📌 **Improves retention** with easy-to-understand explanations
  • 📌 **Tracks progress** so students stay on top of their studies

Technologies Used

  • HTML - Defines the webpage structure
  • CSS - Enhances page styling and responsiveness
  • JavaScript - Adds interactivity and dynamic content
